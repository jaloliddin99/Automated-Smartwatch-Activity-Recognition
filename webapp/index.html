How to Use

Watch Setup

  1. Upload watch/motion-sender.js to your Bangle.js using the Espruino Web IDE
  2. Click Connect Watch and select your Bangle.js
  3. Press the button on the watch to start sending data

Printer Setup (USB)

  1. Connect Ultimaker 2+ to your computer with a USB cable
  2. Click Connect Printer and select the serial port
  3. The printer will home and heat up automatically when you hit Record

Drawing

  1. Click Record — tilt your hand to move the cursor
  2. Hold watch flat = pen DOWN (draws/extrudes)
  3. Raise/tilt watch up = pen UP (moves without drawing)
  4. X axis = left/right, Y axis = forward/back, Z axis = up (sky)
  5. Click Stop when done
  6. Use Export G-code to download as file (fallback for SD card)

No watch? Draw with your mouse on the canvas to test. Printer connection requires Chrome or Edge browser.
